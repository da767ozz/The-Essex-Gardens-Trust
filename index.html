About The Essex Gardens Trust

Organization Summary

The Essex Gardens Trust is a dedicated organization that aims to promote, protect, and enhance the landscape heritage of Essex. Our activities encompass raising awareness, providing education, and advocating for the conservation of gardens and green spaces throughout the region.

Mission

Our mission is to safeguard and promote the garden heritage of Essex for future generations. We strive to educate the public about the importance of gardens and green spaces in our communities while actively participating in conservation efforts.

History

The Essex Gardens Trust was established in 1995 as a response to the growing recognition of the need to protect and preserve our natural and designed landscapes. Over the years, we have worked closely with local authorities, schools, and community groups to foster a greater appreciation for the rich history and diversity of Essex's gardens.
